## Purpose
To prevent pest activity and maintain a clean, sanitary food establishment.

Prevention
- Doors and windows are kept closed or properly screened
- Gaps, cracks, and openings are sealed when identified
- Food and waste are properly stored and removed regularly

Monitoring
- Employees monitor for signs of pest activity including droppings, insects, or gnaw marks
- Any signs of pests are reported immediately to the Person in Charge (PIC)

Response
- The PIC contacts a licensed pest control professional when needed
- No unauthorized pesticides are used inside the facility

Cleaning
- Food debris and spills are cleaned promptly
- Trash is removed daily or as needed

Responsibility
- Employees report pest activity
- PIC ensures corrective action is taken
