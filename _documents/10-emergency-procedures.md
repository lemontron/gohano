## Purpose

To protect food safety during emergencies that may affect normal operations.

Power Outage

- Cold holding units remain closed as much as possible
- Food temperatures are monitored
- TCS food above 41Â°F for more than 4 hours is discarded
- Operations are discontinued if food safety cannot be maintained

Water Interruption

- Food preparation and warewashing are discontinued
- Bottled or approved water may be used only if allowed
- Operations resume only after water service is restored and safe

Sewage Backup or Flooding

- Operation is immediately discontinued
- Exposed food is discarded
- Affected areas are cleaned and sanitized
- Local regulatory authority is contacted

Responsibility

- Employees report emergency conditions
- Person in Charge (PIC) determines corrective actions and when to resume operations
