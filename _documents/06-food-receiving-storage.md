## Purpose

To ensure food received and stored is safe, wholesome, and protected from contamination.

Receiving Food

- Food deliveries are inspected upon arrival
- Cold TCS foods are received at 41°F or below
- Frozen foods are solidly frozen
- Hot foods (if received) are received at 135°F or above
- Damaged, spoiled, or out-of-temperature food is rejected

Storage

- Food is stored immediately after receiving
- Raw foods are stored below ready-to-eat foods
- Food is stored in clean, food-grade containers
- All food is labeled and dated
- FIFO (First In, First Out) is followed

Protection from Contamination

- Food is kept covered when not in use
- Food is stored off the floor
- Separate storage is maintained for raw and ready-to-eat foods

Responsibility

- Employees follow receiving and storage procedures
- Person in Charge (PIC) monitors compliance
