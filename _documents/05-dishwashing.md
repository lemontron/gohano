## Purpose

To ensure pots, pans, and other equiptment are properly cleaned and sanitized before reuse.

## Warewashing Method

Utensils and equipment are washed using an approved warewashing method.

Three-Compartment Sink Procedure

1. **Wash** — Hot water and detergent
2. **Rinse** — Clean, hot water
3. **Sanitize** — Approved sanitizer at proper concentration
4. **Air Dry** — No towel drying

Sanitizing

- Only approved food-contact sanitizers are used
- Sanitizer concentration is checked with test strips
- Proper contact time is observed

Frequency

- Utensils and equipment are washed as needed throughout the day
- All items are washed and sanitized at the end of each shift

Responsibility

- Employees follow proper warewashing procedures
- Person in Charge (PIC) ensures compliance
