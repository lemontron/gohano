## Purpose
To prevent the transfer of harmful bacteria between foods, surfaces, and equipment.

Food Handling

- Ready-to-eat foods are protected from raw foods at all times
- Separate utensils and containers are used for different food types
- Utensils are cleaned and sanitized between tasks

Glove & Utensil Use

- Gloves or utensils are used when handling ready-to-eat foods
- Gloves are changed between tasks and when contaminated
- Gloves do not replace handwashing

Work Surfaces

- Food-contact surfaces are cleaned and sanitized between tasks
- Cutting boards and prep surfaces are task-specific or cleaned between uses

Storage Order

- Raw foods are stored below ready-to-eat foods
- Food is stored in covered, labeled containers

Responsibility

- Employees follow contamination-prevention procedures
- Person in Charge (PIC) ensures compliance
