## Purpose

To prevent food contamination by ensuring food-contact surfaces and equipment are properly cleaned and sanitized.

## Definitions

- **Cleaning:** Removing food debris and grease using detergent and water
- **Sanitizing:** Reducing bacteria to safe levels using an approved sanitizer

### Cleaning & Sanitizing Procedure

Food-contact surfaces and equipment are cleaned and sanitized:

- Before starting food preparation
- Between tasks
- After handling raw food
- At the end of each shift
- As needed to prevent contamination

### Steps:

1. Remove food debris
2. Wash with detergent and warm water
3. Rinse with clean water
4. Apply approved sanitizer at proper concentration
5. Allow required contact time
6. Air dry

### Approved Sanitizers

- Only food-safe, approved sanitizers are used
- Sanitizer concentration is maintained per manufacturer instructions
- Test strips are used to verify concentration
