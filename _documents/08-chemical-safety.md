## Purpose

To prevent chemical contamination of food, food-contact surfaces, and equipment.

Approved Chemicals

- Only approved, food-safe cleaning and sanitizing chemicals are used
- Chemicals are used according to manufacturer instructions

Storage

- Chemicals are stored away from food, utensils, and food-contact surfaces
- Chemicals are never stored above food or food-prep areas
- All chemicals are kept in their original containers or properly labeled containers

Labeling

- All spray bottles and secondary containers are clearly labeled with contents
- Unlabeled chemicals are not used

Use

- Chemicals are never mixed
- Chemicals are not used near exposed food
- Employees wash hands after handling chemicals

Safety Information

- Safety Data Sheets (SDS) are available for all chemicals used

Responsibility

- Employees follow chemical safety procedures
- Person in Charge (PIC) ensures proper storage and labeling
