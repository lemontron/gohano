## Purpose
To prevent the growth of harmful bacteria by maintaining proper time and temperature control of TCS foods.

### Cold Holding
- All cold TCS foods are maintained at 41°F (5°C) or below
- Refrigerators are monitored daily
- Food temperatures are checked using a calibrated food thermometer
- Food found above 41°F for more than 4 hours is discarded

### Hot Holding
- Hot TCS foods are maintained at 135°F or above
- Sushi rice is held hot in an approved rice cooker/rice warmer
- Temperatures are checked periodically during service
- Food below 135°F is discarded

### Cooking Temperatures
If cooking occurs:
- Foods are cooked to required minimum internal temperatures per food code
- Temperatures are verified using a food thermometer

### Reheating
- Do not reheat food, throw it out and make new food

### Thermometer Use
- A food thermometer is available and used to verify temperatures
- Thermometers are cleaned and sanitized between uses

### Responsibility
- Food employees monitor holding temperatures
- Person in Charge (PIC) ensures compliance and corrective action
