## Purpose
To prevent the spread of foodborne illness by ensuring proper handwashing by all employees.

## When Handwashing Is Required
Food employees must wash their hands:
- Before starting work
- Before handling food or clean utensils
- After using the restroom
- After eating, drinking, smoking, or chewing gum
- After handling raw food
- After touching face, hair, or body
- After taking out trash or cleaning
- After handling money or phones
- Any time hands may be contaminated

## Handwashing Method

1. Use designated handwashing sink only
2. Turn on warm water
3. Apply soap
4. Scrub hands and arms up to wrists for at least 20 seconds
5. Rinse thoroughly
6. Dry hands using paper towels or air dryer
7. Use paper towel to turn off faucet

## Glove Use
- Gloves do not replace handwashing
- Hands must be washed before putting on gloves
- Gloves must be changed between tasks

## Responsibility
- All food employees must follow this procedure
- Person in Charge (PIC) ensures compliance
